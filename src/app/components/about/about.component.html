About CUT REGISTRY

It is headed by the Deputy Registrar (Academic) who reports day to day to the Registrar and professionally to the Pro Vice Chancellor (Academic Affairs and Administration). The Department deals with all academic affairs of students, (that is, admissions, registration and records management and examinations) within the student life cycle. The Academic Registry Department operates in partnership with all Schools/Institute and Departments and provides support services to external and internal stakeholders that include the following: - Admission and Registration of Students, Record keeping and management, Administration of Examinations, Timetabling of Examinations, Advisory Services with regards to academic rules and regulations (regulator within the University), Verification of students credentials, Requests from organizations, Regulator within the University, Interpretation of rules and regulations to ensure they are fair and equal to all, Students’ Careers advise, Support for systems processes which underpin academic administration, Attestation letters of degrees, transcripts and proficiency to English.

WHERE WE ARE LOCATED ?

Located in the rich agricultural region of Mashonaland West Province and endowed with resort areas such as Kariba, Manapools, Mazvikadei, Chinhoyi Caves and Biri Dam. Situated less than a kilometre from Chinhoyi Central Business District with major retail outlets (such as, Edgars, JET, OK, TM, N Richards) and very close to the Chinhoyi Provincial Hospital.
